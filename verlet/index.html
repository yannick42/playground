Verlet integration
Goal: play with math & physics equations

Using Verlet integration (1967), "from scratch", simple solver to compute particles' motion (under a downward gravity force), better stability than explicit Euler method (?), without handling velocities only particle positions (current & previous). Collision detection against walls and between balls. Possibly pinned points (red), and stick length constraints to make rigid bodies.
