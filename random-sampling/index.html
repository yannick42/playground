Random sampling
Methods: uniform, Poisson disk sampling, acceptance-rejection method
  • Poisson disk sampling allows to maintain an user-defined minimal spacing between the random points
  • The acceptance-rejection algorithm uses an uniform distribution (JS Math.random()) as the proposal distribution, and use the PDF (density function) of the Gaussian (µ=0, σ=1) as the desired/target distribution (centered on the canvas). It needs two values x and y.
