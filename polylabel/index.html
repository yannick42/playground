Point of Inaccessibility
Goal: find where to place a polygon's label

The 'Polylabel' method, by Mapbox, to find the point of inaccessibility (guaranteed to be the global maximum) using a heap-based priority queue to order cells with potentially maximum distance to polygon border iteratively. Starting from polygon's centroid.
