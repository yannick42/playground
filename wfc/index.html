Wave Function Collapse algorithm
Goal: to generate random content (procedural generation) following constraints

Two modes are possible : tiled (used here) or overlapping
Selecting the next tile to set/collapse, as the one with the lowest possible moves


Model synthesis by Paul Merrell (2007), a previous method (more complex ?) that inspired the WFC popular method (by Maxim Gumin, 2016)
