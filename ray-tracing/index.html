Ray Tracing
Goal: to draw realistic 3D scenes, following Ray tracing in one weekend mini-book series.

Simulating diffuse materials, metal, and dielectrics (glass, water, ...) using Snell's law to compute refracted rays and total internal reflection. Schlick's approximation (1994) is used for the specular reflections...
